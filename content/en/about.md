<h3>Who We Are</h3>
<p>Ground Data Technology Solutions Joint Stock Company (<strong>GDTS</strong>) is a technology company based in Ho Chi Minh City, specializing in the collection, processing, and analysis of geospatial data for businesses, researchers, and management agencies.</p>

<p>Built on a foundation of scientific expertise and field experience, the GDTS team combines UAV technology, GIS, remote sensing, and internationally recognized analysis methods to deliver accurate, transparent, and high-value data.</p>

<h3>Our Services</h3>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Ground Data Collection</strong> – for business development and urban management</li>
  <li><strong>Scientific Data Collection</strong> – soil, water, and air data for research purposes</li>
  <li><strong>Agricultural Data Collection</strong> – using UAVs equipped with Multispectral sensors</li>
  <li><strong>Geospatial Data Analysis</strong> – GIS, remote sensing, thematic mapping, and decision support</li>
  <li><strong>Coffee Carbon Labeling</strong> – carbon footprint measurement to IPCC/ISO standards, with QR traceability</li>
</ul>

<h3>What Sets Us Apart</h3>
<p>GDTS's coffee carbon labeling solution was developed by a representative selected for the <strong>LIF Global 2026 program by the Royal Academy of Engineering (United Kingdom)</strong> — affirming its potential for international application and commercialization.</p>

<p>With a team rooted in scientific research and international publications, GDTS is committed to delivering high-quality data that complies with rigorous standards and satisfies audit requirements from both domestic and international partners.</p>

<h3>Our Mission</h3>
<p>GDTS aims to be a trusted geospatial data technology partner, helping organizations and businesses in Vietnam access, leverage, and apply geospatial data effectively — enabling data-driven strategic decisions with confidence.</p>
