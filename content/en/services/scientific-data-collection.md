<h3>Map, Geology, and Environmental Foundation</h3>

<p>With the map, geology, and environmental foundation of our key members, we are able to plan scientific data collection in an optimal and accurate manner. <h3>Workforce Mobilization</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc-2.webp" alt="Workforce Mobilization" class="responsive">
<p>To obtain accurate and up-to-date <strong>scientific data</strong>, it is essential to mobilize and maintain a sufficient number of data collectors. Understanding that this is one of the key challenges of the information collection process, we are always proactive in recruitment and possess workforce mobilization solutions. Personnel responsible for data collection and processing are formally trained in cartography, geology, and environmental sciences. As a result, we are fully capable of resolving issues that arise during data collection and addressing factors that affect data accuracy during inspection.</p>
<h3>Workflow Consulting</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc-3.webp" alt="Workflow Consulting" class="responsive">
<p>GDTS will carry out the field data collection process according to the workflow provided by the client. If the client does not yet have an established workflow, we will develop a preliminary plan and consult on the working process after receiving all requirements. This process will take place with the client's direct participation to ensure the quality of data collected in the field.</p>
<h3>Quality Assurance and After-sales Support</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc/thu-thap-du-lieu-khoa-hoc-4.webp" alt="Quality Assurance and After-sales Support" class="responsive">
<p>Having originated as scientists and possessing peer-reviewed publications both domestically and internationally, GDTS understands that the quality of collected data will greatly affect your research results. Therefore, we guarantee compliance with <strong>scientific data collection</strong> methods according to general standards. If you have high-standard requirements that need to be met, we are fully capable of providing support. After product handover, the company will decide, based on the type and volume of data, to support the collection and provision of the most up-to-date data for a number of critical locations in the research.</p>
