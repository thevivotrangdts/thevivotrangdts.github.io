<h2>About</h2>
<h3><span>Ground Data Collection</span> Service</h3>
</div>

<div class="content">
<p><strong>Welcome. Are you looking for Ground Data collection solutions for business expansion and development purposes? Or do you need to collect actual field information for rural and urban management work? Ground Data Technology Solutions Joint Stock Company (GDTS) is fully capable of supporting you.</strong></p>
<br>
<h3>Map Foundation</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-1.webp" alt="Map Foundation" class="responsive">
<p>With the map foundation of our key members, we are able to plan field data collection optimally. As a result, the company understands the main factors that may affect the quality of collected data and always prepares methods to minimize those impacts during field collection as well as during data processing.</p>
<h3>Workforce Mobilization</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-2.webp" alt="Workforce Mobilization" class="responsive">
<p>To obtain the most up-to-date and fastest field data, it is essential to mobilize and maintain a sufficient number of data collectors. Understanding that this is one of the key challenges of the information collection process, we are always proactive in recruitment and possess workforce mobilization solutions. Personnel responsible for data collection and processing are formally trained in cartography. As a result, we are fully capable of resolving issues that arise during data collection and addressing problems affecting data quality during inspection.</p>
<h3>Workflow Consulting</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-3.webp" alt="Workflow Consulting" class="responsive">
<p>GDTS will carry out the <strong>ground data collection</strong> process according to the workflow provided by the client. If the client does not yet have an established workflow, we will develop a plan and consult on the working process after receiving all requirements. This process will take place with the client's direct participation to ensure the quality of data collected in the field.</p>
<h3>Quality Assurance and Data Warranty</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-4.webp" alt="Quality Assurance and Data Warranty" class="responsive">
<p>In parallel with the field data collection process, we will conduct quality control. Committed to prioritizing the quality of data delivered to clients, GDTS focuses on building an optimized and accurate daily data inspection process that meets the stated requirements. The quality control team is selected and trained to fully understand each client's data needs. After product handover, we will provide different warranty packages based on the type and volume of data.</p>
<h3>Quantity and Quality Reporting</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-5.webp" alt="Quantity and Quality Reporting" class="responsive">
<p>We understand the importance of statistics in project management. To ensure maximum convenience for clients, GDTS will report the quantity and quality of collected data according to each client's usage needs. At a minimum, the company will report the daily collection volume at the end of each day and report dataset quality after inspection at the end of each week.</p>
<h3>Data Collection Software</h3>
<img src="/assets/img/product/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat/thu-thap-du-lieu-mat-dat-6.webp" alt="Data Collection Software" class="responsive">
<p>Finally, the company is capable of building a cross-platform <strong>ground data collection</strong> application (Android, iOS). This software will help field collectors perform their work conveniently. Through this tool, we can limit the fields and types of information that can be collected, thereby reducing errors in the field. Thanks to a consistent data structure, quality control is carried out more efficiently and accurately.</p>
